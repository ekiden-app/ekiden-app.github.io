Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Data Collection

Ekiden does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no network requests of any kind.

Where Your Data Lives

All your media library data — titles, tags, notes, progress, cover images — is stored exclusively on your Mac in Ekiden's local application container. It never leaves your device.

Network Access

Ekiden does not connect to the internet. The app makes no outbound network requests. No data is sent anywhere, ever.

Third Parties

Ekiden does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external APIs.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes — for example, if a future version adds an optional feature that involves network access — it will be updated here and noted in the app's release notes. The date above will reflect the most recent update.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can reach out via GitHub.
